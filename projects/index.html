Travel by Design

Travel by Design, a trip planner assistant, is a mobile application that our external sponsor, Lyndea Dew, had assigned our group to complete in 10 weeks. Travel by Design’s goal is to help make the process of planning a trip less overwhelming. When planning a trip, there are often many factors involved: tours to go on, museums to visit, shopping venues to shop at, trails to hike, and cultural spots that can’t be missed.
