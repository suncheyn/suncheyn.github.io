Competitive Analysis

The main competitors of CDT are centers of research and excellence that focus on similar missions and target similar audiences such as:

  • MIT Initiative on the Digital Economy
  • MIT Center for Information Systems

MIT Initiative on the Digital Economy (IDE) is effective in clearly stating their mission statement directly on their landing page. They also include a huge Call to Action that allows users to learn more. The News, Events, and Media section below the header brings attention to the most current events happening within IDE.
